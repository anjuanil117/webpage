WELCOME TO ONLINE QUIZ.....























INFORMATIONS

1. Each Quiz will contain ten rounds with ten questions in each round.
2. One point will be awarded for each correct answer.
3.No half marks will be awarded.
4.Each question will be asked twice.
5.Each team will have five seconds to write its answer after which time a bell will ring.
6.The question master will then proceed to the next question in the round.
7.The answer sheets will be collected after each round.
8.Any query regarding a question must be made by a member of the competing team prior to the announcement of the result.

RULES

1. Each competitor must be over 16 on 1st January in the year of the All-Ireland Final of the competition.Each Quiz will contain ten rounds with ten questions in each round. 2.The competition will be organised as a Table Quiz.
3. The number of participants on each team shall be five (four plus one substitute). A maximum of four and a minimum of three participants must sit at the table. If less than three members of team are present at the commencement of a competition, that team may not participate in the competition.
4. All paper for use in the Quiz will be provided by the Organising Committee.
5. No books or paper other than that provided by the Organising Committee will be allowed.
6. No conferring will be allowed between teams during the Quiz.
7. A master score sheet must be provided for each Quiz and must be on public display.
8. Two scorekeepers shall be employed at all levels of the competition.
9. In the event of a draw, the teams will be asked five questions. If there is still a draw, a further five questions will be asked, and in the event of a continuing tie, the teams involved will be asked single questions until a winner emerges.
10. If a Centre which won the All-Ireland Final in the previous year wishes to participate, it must present a team that does not include any member of the previous year's team.
11. The four teams with the highest scores at each provincial final shall be eligible to compete in the All-Ireland Final.
12. The All-Ireland Final shall be completed by April 30th.
